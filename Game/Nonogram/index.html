How to Play Picross
  1. Nonograms (Picross) are picture logic puzzles where cells in a grid must be filled or left blank according to numbers at the side of the grid.
  2. The numbers tell you the length of consecutive groups of filled squares. For example, a hint of "3 1" means there is a group of three filled cells, followed by at least one empty cell, followed by a single filled cell.
  3. Left Click & Drag to paint filled squares.
  4. Right Click & Drag to mark squares with an X (to show you know they are empty).
  5. On mobile, use the Fill / Cross active toggle buttons to switch between painting blocks and marking X's.
  6. Completing a row or column correctly will automatically dim its hints to help you keep track of your progress!
