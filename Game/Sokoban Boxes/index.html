Warehouse Operations Protocol

Welcome to Industrial Logic Systems logistics bay. As a certified warehouse operator, you are tasked with organizing shipping containers.

Safety Guidelines:

  • You can only push boxes forward. You cannot pull boxes.
  • You can only push one box at a time. Pushing two adjacent boxes is mechanically impossible.
  • Place all boxes onto designated target bays (the gold concentric circles) to complete the floor protocol.
